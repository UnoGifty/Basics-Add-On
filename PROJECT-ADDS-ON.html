GRAPHIC DESIGN

My Favorite hobby

Graphic design is the practice of using visuals to communicate ideas. From product packaging to social media posts, graphic designers combine color, texture, shapes, and typography to create designs that are equal parts eye-catching and purposeful. It's common to assume graphic design is only about aesthetics. But at its core, graphic design is about clarity.
